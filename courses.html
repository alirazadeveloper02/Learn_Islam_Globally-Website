Our Courses

Courses We Offer

Getting yourself or your loved ones familiarized with the teachings of the holy Quran doesn’t have to be a hassle. This is the reason why Quran Learn Academy has developed an extremely easy to understand online registration process you can go through to get yourself or your children registered. Just follow these steps to get things on the way.

Qaida for Beginners

A comprehensive foundational course designed specifically for beginners, guiding them step by step through the Arabic alphabet, correct pronunciation, and essential rules of Quranic reading, with clear explanations, easy-to-follow lessons, and a focus on building confidence and accuracy in every stage of learning.

Quran Reading

We make Quran learning easy, comfortable, and accessible through our interactive online system, guided by experienced and dedicated tutors who provide personalized attention to every student. Our approach focuses on nurturing spiritual development, strengthening academic growth, and building confidence in Quranic understanding, all within a supportive and engaging learning environment

Hifz ul Quran

Our online Quran memorization course helps students achieve Hifz with expert guidance, offering a structured and step-by-step approach to word-by-word memorization. Under the supervision of skilled and experienced teachers, students receive personalized support to ensure accuracy, consistency, and strong long-term retention, while developing discipline, focus, and a deep spiritual connection with the Holy Quran.

Quran Translation

A comprehensive and thoughtfully structured course designed to help learners understand the meanings of Quranic verses through clear, word-by-word translation and meaningful spiritual reflection. The course enables students to grasp the depth of Allah’s message, strengthen their connection with the Quran, and apply its guidance with clarity and awareness in everyday life.

Tafseer ul Quran

An in-depth and reflective course that explores the meanings, historical context, and profound wisdom behind Quranic verses, helping learners develop a deeper understanding and heartfelt connection with the message of the Holy Quran. The course encourages thoughtful reflection, spiritual growth, and practical application of Quranic guidance in daily life.

Quran With Tajweed

Ahsan Islamic Institute offers Expert-led Quran Tajweed courses designed to help you improve and perfect your recitation through well-structured lessons and clear, step-by-step guidance. With the support of experienced and dedicated instructors, students develop correct pronunciation, proper application of Tajweed rules, and confidence in recitation.

New Muslim Course

A supportive and beginner-friendly course specially tailored for new Muslims, covering the fundamentals of Islam, daily prayers, Quran reading, and essential beliefs. Designed to provide clarity, confidence, and encouragement, this course helps learners build a strong spiritual foundation and smoothly begin their journey of faith with understanding and ease.

Seerat un Nabi

A heart-touching course that explores the life, character, and noble teachings of Prophet Muhammad (Peace Be Upon Him) in depth, highlighting his compassion, wisdom, and exemplary conduct. The course offers timeless guidance and practical inspiration for everyday life, helping learners strengthen their faith, refine their character, and apply Prophetic teachings in personal, social, and spiritual matters.

Dars e Nizami

A comprehensive traditional Islamic studies course covering core subjects such as Fiqh, Hadith, Tafseer, and Arabic grammar, thoughtfully designed to build strong scholarly foundations. The course nurtures a deep understanding of Islamic knowledge through structured learning, classical methodologies, and guided instruction, helping students develop clarity in belief, practice, and academic insight.
