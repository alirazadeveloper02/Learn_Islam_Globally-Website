Daily Duas

Daily Duas Learn Authentic Islamic Supplications

Join our Daily Duas course to learn essential Islamic supplications for everyday life morning, evening, travel, and more.

بِسْمِ اللّٰہِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيْمِ

Dua Before Eating

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ وَعَلَى بَرَكَةِ اللَّهِ

In the name of Allah and with the blessings of Allah.

Dua Before Eating

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَطْعَمَنَا وَسَقَانَا وَجَعَلَنَا مِنَ الْمُسْلِمِينَ

All praise is for Allah who fed us and gave us drink and made us the Muslims.

Dua After Eating

اللَّهُمَّ بِاسْمِكَ أَمُوتُ وَأَحْيَا

O Allah, with Your name I die and live.

Dua Before Sleep

اللَّهُـمَّ إِنِّي أَعُـوذُ بِـكَ مِـنَ الْخُـبْثِ وَالْخَبَائِثِ.

O Allah, I seek refuge in You.

Dua Before Entering the Bathroom

الحَمْـدُ لِلّهِ الّذي أَحْـيانا بَعْـدَ ما أَماتَـنا وَإليه النُّـشور

All praise belongs to Allah Who has restored us back to life after causing us to die; and to Him shall we return.

Dua for Waking Up

غُفْرَانَكَ.

I ask You (Allah) for forgiveness

Dua When Leaving the Bathroom

رَّبِّ زِدْنِيْ عِلْمًا

O my Lord, increase my knowledge.

Dua When Leaving the Bathroom

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَذْهَبَ عَنِّي الْأَذَى

All praise is for Allah who removed harm from me.

Dua When Leaving the Bathroom

اللَّهُـمَّ إِنِّي أَعُـوذُ بِـكَ مِـنَ الْخُـبْثِ وَالْخَبَائِثِ

O Allah, I take refuge with you from all evil and evil-doers.

Dua When Leaving the Bathroom

سُبْحـانَكَ اللّهُـمَّ وَبِحَمدِك، أَشْهَـدُ أَنْ لا إِلهَ إِلاّ أَنْتَ أَسْتَغْفِرُكَ وَأَتوبُ إِلَـيْك

Glory is to You, O Allah, and praise is to You. I bear witness that there is none worthy of worship but You. I seek Your forgiveness and repent to You.

At the End of a Gathering/Majlis

رَبَّنَا اصْرِفْ عَنَّا عَذَابَ جَهَنَّمَ ۖ إِنَّ عَذَابَهَا كَانَ غَرَامًا إِنَّهَا سَاءَتْ مُسْتَقَرًّا وَمُقَامًا

Our Lord! Avert from us the Wrath of Hell, for its Wrath is indeed an affliction grievous,- Evil indeed is it as an abode, and as a place to rest in.

For Jannah
